Movie Webpage Project

Context

In this project, me and my partner used our knowledge about HTML and CSS to create a webpage that advertises a fictional movie.

Content

The fake movie that me and my partner created was a action movie called "The Last Move". In "The Last Move" you could hear the sound of chaos in the air, and you could seefuming flames cover everything on the ground. In the sky there is war happening, and two enemies stand face to face with each other, Milo and Blake. They have a long and brutal battle and both are desperate to finish this fight. So they use all of their energy to make their next and hopefully last move to finally finish this rivalry.
